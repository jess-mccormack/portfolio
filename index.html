{Hello}

I am a native Atlanta girl, with a few stops in New York,LA and Texas along the way. I am an experienced graphic designer and digital producer, working in large advertising agencies and small boutique creative firms for the last 15 years. Having been on the management and creative side of the web for most of my career, I am thrilled to be rolling up my sleeves and turning to towards development! I love being able to fuse my organization skills and creative eye to develop beautiful web products. I am currently proficient in web design, HTML and CSS. Outside of work I am a travel junkie. In 2017 I traveled around the world with Remote Year. When I am home, I love to cook and snuggle with my cat Sailor. I am also learning embroidery, which is a very grown up way to hoard friendship bracelet string.
Before you ask - my favorite place was Mexico City. Go.
